About

TFLink (tflink.net), which uniquely provides comprehensive and highly accurate information on transcription factor - target gene interactions, nucleotide sequences and genomic locations of transcription factor binding sites for human and five model organisms (mouse, rat, Drosophila melanogaster, Caenorhabditis elegans, and Saccharomyces cerevisiae). Our aim was to create a database that contains clearly identified data, and provide information about the sources: databases, experimental methods and publications. Therefore, we examined the freely available large transcription factor databases, and selected nine resources for integration: GTRD, HTRIdb, JASPAR, ORegAnno, REDfly, ReMap, TRED, TRRUST, and Yeastract. From the above listed source databases the website of HTRIdb and ORegAnno are no longer available, but the data are still available on our TFLink website. We integrated accurate, low-throughput experimental data, besides the less accurate high-throughput CHIP-seq data without mixing these two types of sources.
